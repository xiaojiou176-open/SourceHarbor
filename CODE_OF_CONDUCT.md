# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the SourceHarbor community a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that build an open, welcoming, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- showing empathy and respect toward other people
- giving and accepting constructive feedback
- taking responsibility and learning from mistakes
- focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language
- trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- publishing other people's private information without permission
- any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct and may take appropriate corrective action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

## Reporting

To report unacceptable behavior, use the repository support routes in
[SUPPORT.md](./SUPPORT.md). For safety-sensitive reports, do not open a public
thread; use the private GitHub reporting path described in
[SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) when the concern involves repository-hosted spaces
or maintainer-controlled project operations.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
